About Us

A community for those who are passionate about cyber security.

A community for those who are passionate about cyber security.

Arcane Society is a cyber security enthusiast-focused CTF team and peer-to-peer community. It is a student-led organisation that operates to collaborate with students who are interested in learning more about cybersecurity and to expose them to the area through industry professionals, CTFs, and alumni.

  • We're a group of folks who enjoy participating in CTFs.
  • We allow you to accomplish your best job on your own time.
  • Join us and let's have a good time.

  • CTF Challenges

    Capture the Flag (CTF) is a special kind of information security competitions. There are three common types of CTFs: Jeopardy, Attack-Defence and mixed. Jeopardy-style CTFs has a couple of questions (tasks) in range of categories. For example, Web, Forensic, Crypto, Binary or something else.

  • Cyber Security & Penetration Testing Courses

    Penetration testing is also known as pen testing or ethical hacking. It describes the intentional launching of simulated cyberattacks that seek out exploitable vulnerabilities in computer systems, networks, websites, and applications.

    Workshops on cyber ethics, email hacking and security, virus assaults, Windows system attacks, online data investigation, credit card frauds and cases, Google hacking, Android mobile hacking, and more.
